Learn how DoorSkill handles fees, guarantees escrow payment safety, and ensures fair pricing for both clients and professionals.

Our Pricing Philosophy

DoorSkill is built around transparency. Posting jobs on DoorSkill is completely free. Providers submit competitive bids, giving clients full freedom to pick the best rate that fits their budget. No hidden fees, no sudden surprises.

For Clients

Free Posting

You pay only the bid amount you accept. The money is securely placed in escrow and released only when you confirm satisfaction.

For Workers

10% Commission

A standard 10% marketplace commission is charged only on successful bookings. You pay nothing until you successfully earn.
