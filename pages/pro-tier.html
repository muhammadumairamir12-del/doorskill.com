Frequently Asked Questions
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period, and you won't be charged for the next cycle.
Do I get a free trial? ▼
Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for all new Pro subscribers. Try all features risk-free before your first charge.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? ▼
Absolutely! You can change your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the difference will be prorated and charged immediately. If you downgrade, the credit will be applied to your next billing cycle.
