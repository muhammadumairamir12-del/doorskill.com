SEARCH & RANKING ALGORITHM
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

7-Factor Ranking System:
  1. Seller Level (25%)       - Pro sellers rank higher
  2. Rating (25%)             - 4.9+ gets top ranking
  3. Conversion Rate (20%)    - Orders completed successfully
  4. Response Time (10%)      - <15 min gets full points
  5. Completion Rate (10%)    - On-time delivery
  6. Recency (5%)             - New gigs get slight boost
  7. Relevance (5%)           - Match user preferences

Filters Available:
  ✅ Category
  ✅ Price Range
  ✅ Delivery Time
  ✅ Seller Rating
  ✅ Seller Level
  ✅ Reviews Count


ORDER STATUSES & FLOW
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Pending          - Buyer ordered, awaiting seller acceptance
2. Accepted         - Seller confirmed order
3. In Progress      - Work in progress on order
4. Submitted        - Seller submitted deliverables
5. Under Review     - Buyer reviewing work
6. Completed        - Order finished, payment released
7. Cancelled        - Order cancelled by buyer or seller

Escrow Management:
  ✅ Funds held securely during order
  ✅ Auto-release on completion
  ✅ Refund on cancellation
  ✅ Dispute resolution ready
