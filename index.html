About DoorSkill

Empowering Local Professionals Globally

DoorSkill is an innovative marketplace connecting everyday people with highly skilled, verified professionals in their local communities.

Our Mission

We believe that finding reliable help for any task should be seamless, secure, and fast. Whether you need a trusted plumber in Multan, a math tutor in Lahore, or an IT expert across the world, DoorSkill brings them directly to your digital doorstep.

Why We Built This

Traditional hiring is slow and lacks transparency. DoorSkill introduces an intelligent bidding system, rigorous identity verification (CNIC & Live Capture), and integrated escrow payments to ensure that every job is completed with maximum trust and safety.
