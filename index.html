About DoorSkill

Empowering Local Trade Professionals

DoorSkill is an innovative marketplace connecting everyday people with highly skilled, verified trade professionals in their local communities.

Our Mission

To empower global and local communities by building a seamless, secure connection between clients and verified professionals. We are committed to making expert services universally accessible, transparent, and reliable for everyone, everywhere. By bridging this gap, we elevate local economies and simplify daily life.

Why We Built This

Finding dependable help shouldn't be stressful or risky, and skilled tradespeople deserve fair opportunities to thrive. We built DoorSkill to replace outdated directory channels with real-time bidding, rigorous CNIC background checks, and escrow protection. Our platform delivers peace of mind, quality assurance, and complete payment security for both parties.
