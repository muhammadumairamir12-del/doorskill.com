🚀 Step-by-Step: How to Register as a Worker

Onboarding as a DoorSkill partner is straightforward. Follow these steps to set up your digital shop and complete the DoorSkill worker registration process:

  1. Signup: Go to the register as worker page and verify your phone number using the Firebase Phone Authentication SMS code.
  2. Verify CNIC: Submit clear photos of your front-and-back CNIC. Complete the quick, live facial capture screen. This is crucial for matching you with high-paying projects.
  3. Define Skills & Rate: Select your category, highlight your skills, and write an engaging bio. Set your hourly rate or base service price.
  4. Go Live: Once approved by our team, your profile becomes searchable. You will instantly start receiving alerts for jobs posted in your area and earn money without degree Pakistan restrictions if you have the practical skills.
