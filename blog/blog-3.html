For decades, getting any task done around the house in Pakistan meant playing phone tag with middle-men, pleading with technicians to show up on time, and haggling endlessly over arbitrary costs. There was no single platform where you could compare options, check work history, or guarantee that you wouldn't get scammed. Homeowners were forced to accept whatever price was quoted, and hope the handyman was skilled enough not to make the problem worse.

What is DoorSkill? DoorSkill is Pakistan's leading online marketplace that connects clients with verified local professionals for any skill or service — from plumbers to doctors to tutors.

DoorSkill completely reimagines this process. By introducing a job bidding platform model, we put the power back in the hands of the consumer. On DoorSkill, instead of chasing workers, they come to you. This tutorial provides a complete walkthrough of how you can post a job Pakistan and receive competitive bids in just a few minutes, making online service booking a breeze.

📋 Step 1 — Create Your Free Account

To begin, simply head over to the signup page. Registering is completely free and takes less than a minute. You can sign up and hire worker online using your phone number. Standard SMS OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to verify your identity. During registration, make sure to select the "Client" profile if you want to hire professionals, or the "Worker" profile if you want to offer your services.

✍️ Step 2 — Post Your Job (Title, Budget, Location)

Once logged in, click on the **Post a Job** button. You will be presented with our quick job posting form where you will define the details of your project on the local job platform:

  • Title: Keep it short and descriptive (e.g., "Install PPR Pipe in Kitchen" or "Hire Electrician for Ceiling Fan Installation").
  • Category: Choose from Home Repairs, Cleaning, Beauty, Auto, Logistics, or select "Other" for custom requests.
  • Description: Explain the problem clearly. Mention any specific details, like the type of geyser or height of the ceiling. The more detail you provide, the more accurate the bids will be.
  • Budget: Set a flexible starting budget. This lets workers know your expectations when you post job free Pakistan.
  • Location: Enter your city and area. Our service provider app Pakistan uses geolocation to notify workers closest to your home.

📬 Step 3 — Receive Bids from Nearby Workers

As soon as you submit the form, our automated system matches your requirements with qualified, verified specialists in your immediate neighborhood. They receive an instant alert on their worker dashboards. Interested specialists will inspect your request and submit their pricing bids. Within minutes, you will get bids online Pakistan on your job marketplace Pakistan dashboard. This competitive bidding ensures you get the best bid service without haggling.

📷 Visual Tip

Adding clear photos of the issue (like a leaky sink trap or a damaged socket) helps workers understand the scope immediately, leading to more accurate, lower price quotes.

🔍 Step 4 — Compare Profiles, Ratings & Prices

With quotes in hand, it is time to make an informed decision. Don't just pick the lowest bid. Take a moment to compare worker bids by clicking on their profiles:

  1. Check their average star rating and read past client reviews.
  2. Review their portfolio of completed tasks and check their listed skills.
  3. Look for the green "Verified" badge, confirming their CNIC checks are complete.

💬 Step 5 — Chat, Confirm & Get It Done

Once you narrow down your choices, use DoorSkill's built-in chat to discuss the details. You can hire online Pakistan and chat or call the professional directly in-app. Ask questions like: "Will you bring the PPR materials, or should I purchase them?" or "What time can you arrive?" Once both parties are aligned, accept the bid and confirm the booking schedule. The professional will arrive at your home at the agreed-upon time.

⭐ Step 6 — Pay Safely & Leave a Review

When the job is complete, test the work to ensure everything functions perfectly. If you are satisfied, release the funds from your escrow dashboard. Finally, leave an honest rating and review. Sharing your feedback is crucial—it helps maintain the quality standards of our community and helps other clients make the right choice.
