Trusting the man, Bilal paid the money. The worker walked out to buy the materials—and never returned. To make matters worse, the electrician left several bare wires exposed in the study, which short-circuited later that evening, causing a small electrical fire that ruined Bilal’s desktop computer.

🔒 Reason 1: Identity Verified (CNIC & Live Capture)

When you hire an undocumented worker, you have no way to verify their true identity. If theft, damage, or inappropriate behavior occurs in your home, you have no recourse. Verified professionals on DoorSkill must undergo a complete national identity verification process. This includes matching their government-issued CNIC (Computerized National Identity Card) against verified records and passing a live facial recognition check. Hiring identity verified workers who are fully documented and traceable provides an essential layer of security for your family.

⚡ Reason 2: Skill-Tested Before Approval

Amateur handymen often use guesswork instead of diagnostic training. They might fix a pipe using cheap tape that bursts under pressure three days later, or wire a three-phase AC connection incorrectly. Verified workers on platforms like DoorSkill go through a verification check where their past credentials, training certificates, or references are evaluated. This skilled verified labor screening ensures that when you request a service, you receive a specialist who understands standard operating procedures, possesses the correct tools, and does the job right the first time.

💰 Reason 3: Escrow Payment — You Pay Only After Job Done

The "advance payment scam" is one of the most common issues in the local service sector. Workers take advance cash to buy parts and vanish. DoorSkill eliminates this risk completely through a secure escrow payment Pakistan system. When you accept a professional's bid, your payment is placed safely in a secure digital vault. The money remains there while the work is being performed. It is only released to the worker when you click "Release Payment" on your dashboard, confirming you are 100% satisfied with the outcome.

💡 Trust Tip

Never pay any service provider in cash prior to job completion. Utilizing in-app escrow protects your funds and ensures the provider has a strong incentive to deliver quality work.

⭐ Reason 4: Ratings & Reviews Are Real

In traditional hiring, a technician's reputation is based entirely on self-promotion. They will tell you they are the best plumber in Karachi, but you have no way to double-check. On DoorSkill, reviews are 100% authentic and linked directly to completed transactions. A worker cannot post fake reviews or delete negative feedback. You can browse their profile to read real, uncensored experiences and check verified worker ratings Pakistan statistics from previous clients in your neighborhood, helping you make a data-backed hiring decision.

💬 Reason 5: In-App Chat — No Personal Number Sharing

Privacy is a major concern, particularly for female homeowners and families. Sharing your personal mobile phone or WhatsApp number with a stranger exposes you to potential follow-up calls or spam messages. DoorSkill protects your privacy by offering fully integrated, encrypted in-app messaging and calling. You can coordinate your appointment, share pictures of the repair area, and negotiate pricing without ever sharing your private contact details.
